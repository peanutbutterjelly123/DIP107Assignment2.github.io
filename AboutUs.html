Our Background

Welcome to EYSI, where passion for fragrances led to the creation of our online shop. Inspired by the captivating power of scents, our founder, Evelyn, embarked on a journey to curate the finest collection of candles, fragrance room sprays, and essential oils.

With a commitment to quality and craftsmanship, we invite you to explore our hand-poured candles, carefully crafted using premium soy waxes and infused with exquisite fragrances.

Let our products transport you to a world of tranquility and bliss as you indulge in the sensory delights we have to offer.
